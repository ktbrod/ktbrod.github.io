How to Play Operation Equilibrium

Welcome to Operation Equilibrium. Your goal is to keep a balance between EMOTION and LOGIC.

  1. Use the controls shown on the main game page to adjust emotion and logic.

    Keep balance meter between 40 and 60 for a perfect score.

  2. If you incorporate too much emotion (leaning towards end of meter) the system destabilizes and destructs!
  3. Keep the levels near the center (logic) of each lever to survive as long as possible.

    4. Try to keep your balance less than 60 and more than 40 for a perfect score!
