Boundary Conditions – Human Path

The System Reads You as “More Human”

The system calls you “human”.

It sees hesitation where you see reflection,

inefficiency where you see imagination.

To drift is not to fail a task.

It is to refuse optimization as the only logic of movement.

Your slowness leaves room for memory,

your pauses create a space for doubt,

and doubt is a kind of freedom.
