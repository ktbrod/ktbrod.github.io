Billionaire Playground

    • About

      This project is meant serve as a commentary on our timeline in which only the uber wealthy can realistically 'go to space', and how they alone can profit off of the mysteries beyond.

      It is a commentary on the idea that money is no longer viewed as a necessity or a tool by some, but more so a muse

      It is a commentary on the out of touch nature of some of the worlds most wealthy individuals

      How to play

      Use the W, A, S, D Keys to move the ship towards the falling space material to 'collect' for profit.

      For mobile devices with gyro sensors, select 'enable motion' for an enhanced viewing experience.

      To exit and display the menu bar, press the escape key
