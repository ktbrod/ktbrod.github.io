How to Play

  1. Press "Let's Go!" to begin!
  2. Listen to the audio. You can replay this as many times as you need!
  3. Try to listen for intervals and notice if notes are getting higher or lower.
  4. Select the musical phrase that best matches what you heard. Repeat for all 5 questions.
  5. Play the game as many times as need to get your high score!
